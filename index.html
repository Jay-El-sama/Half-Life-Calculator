How to Use the Half-Life Calculator

  • Initial Amount: Enter the starting quantity of the substance (e.g., 100 grams).
  • Elapsed Time: How much time has passed since decay started (e.g., 24 days).
  • Half-Life: Time it takes for half the substance to decay (e.g., 6 days).
  • Units: Choose appropriate time and mass units to match your data.
  • Click "Calculate": You'll see how much of the substance is left.

Example: If you start with 100 mg of a substance and its half-life is 10 hours, after 10 hours you'll have 50 mg. After 20 hours, 25 mg. And so on.
